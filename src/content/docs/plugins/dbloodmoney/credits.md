**dBloodMoney** — kill a player, take their money.

## Editions

- **Free** — `dBloodMoney.jar`: kill-to-steal + full anti-abuse suite.
- **PRO** — `dBloodMoney-PRO.jar`: adds bounties, leaderboards, kill streaks and PlaceholderAPI.

When reporting an issue, include:

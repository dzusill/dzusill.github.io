**dBloodMoney** turns every kill into a payday. When a player kills another, the killer steals part of the victim's balance — a high-stakes economy mechanic for PvP, factions, KitPvP and survival servers.

It is built on [DzusillCore](https://github.com/dzusill/DzusillCore) and ships in two editions from the same plugin: a free core and a **PRO** upgrade that adds bounties, leaderboards and kill streaks.

---

## What it does

- 💰 **Kill to steal** — the killer takes a configurable percentage of the victim's money, with minimum and maximum limits.
- 🛡️ **Anti-abuse built in** — farm cooldowns, combat tagging, combat-log punishment and same-IP alt blocking, so the mechanic can't be farmed.
- 🎯 **Bounties** *(PRO)* — players put a price on each other's heads; the killer collects.
- 🏆 **Leaderboards** *(PRO)* — track top killers, earnings and best streaks.
- 🔥 **Kill streaks** *(PRO)* — consecutive kills multiply the reward.
- 🧩 **PlaceholderAPI** *(PRO)* — show stats on scoreboards, tab and chat.
